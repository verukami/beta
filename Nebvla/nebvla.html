Discovery

The initial idea for this platform was solve RH challenges. We started to think from HR perspective of the process they needed to go through to hire a new employee and keep their history easily managed and in just one place. I was asked to take on a creative role for this project. I was responsible to release all of the design efforts which included buildid a meaningful brand, designing the user interface & experience.

Problem

1. After watch the participants using their actual tool I found a lot of problems on the user journey for each task that they need to accomplish,. The actual tool is pretty slow, boring and difficult to find sections or to make an useful search. And at the same time it was difficult to administrate the different organziations that need to exist for the admin of the company.

2. The second problem was to create different payrolls for each organization, company or team so I need to fin a way that become easy to create and admin companies that it was capable to grown together with the companies through modules that adapt according to their needs.

3. The last problem was that sometimes the payroll employees need to travel a lot to the different companies that they got in their control, so sometimes it was a little difficult to keep a good communication with employees or the Human Reources area, because the actual system doesn’t got an portable app, that’s why we need to adapat our software with the most important sections to a mobile app, and let us find HR and Payroll be in control of their principal task from everywhere at anytime.

Research

The method that we used was create a session of Design Thinking where in 3 days we try to identified all the profile of the different people involved for the use of the software, understand better their paint points and the needs of our differents user,

I notice that the problem happen when in the different areas need to administrate their personal, most of the time it was due to lack of communication during the processes.

Branding

1. Logo

The brand needed to represent the name of the company and at the same time show that is a mexican product without falling in clichés. Midnight blue as primary color mixed with greens, magentas and cyans become the color palette. In addition we use a mix of geometrical structures with a nebulae inside for the logo.

Also was importat to find the perfect typography choices that would complement de color choices. I settled for Quicksand and Roboto after testing various typeface options. The contrast of these fonts aligned with the profesional approach of the dashboard.

2. Brand guidelines

We create different guidelines for print material and for digital materials with the same palette colour in order to make a very formal and modern brand.

3. Landing Page

Taking the brand guidelines in mind, and already definig what are going to be the principal characteristics of the product that client want to highlight, we work on create the strcuture, wireframe and final screens for the landing page.

Creation

User flows

I create different flowcharts grouped in the actions for each section and for the different permissions that exisit in the platform to show the complete journey that user is going to follow to complete each task that the tool is going to help them.

Final thoughts

This was my first project as a Product Design and the one where I learn a lot of things and let me grow a lot as a designer.

I really understand the importance of testing in each stage of the design process to keep resolving the problems that user can find on the flows. At the same time is pretty important to motivate the user to participate in de Design Thinking to really resolve all their concerns and pain point that they have, and got a correct balance between the things that the company want and what is that users really need.

The importance of define the style guides for the components and got the technology that the final tool is going to use and really listen the suggestions of the developers in mind all the time.

The things that I have been done different was to test for the beginning with our real users and document always the entire process. At the same time I would have done better research methods that let us save time and effort in design.
