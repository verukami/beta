    Melifloz

    Wear Saree The Way You Want. Walk. Run. Dance

    Melifloz is a tech-enabled premium fashion brand for sarees. Mefliz got a Virtual Trail Room that allows users to try product instantly before buying them.

    Role

    UI Designer


    Deliverables

    Product strategy
    Branding
    UI Design


    Team

    Krieger


    Year

    2019

    The challenge.

    Design for scratch an e-commerce for people interested in buying sarees around the world. Melifloz is create for modern woman thst like to bring an iconic element for the culture in new modern ways.

    User Persona

    1. After watching the participants using their actual tool I found a lot of problems on the user journey for each task that they need to accomplish. The actual tool is pretty slow, boring and difficult to find sections or to make a useful search. And at the same time, it was difficult to administrate the different organizations that need to exist for the admin of the company.

    Process

    We interview 25 people about their shoping habits online, what were there pain points and what are the activities that they enjoyed the most, in this form we will be available to learn about the architecture that our compitetors have in their sites.

    Additionally to the design of the interface we create a moodboard to keep in mind what the clients and user was looking for Melifloz.

    Branding

    After some itinerations the final logo is a clean solid form in a black background to create an elegant and minimalistic aestethic.

    For other itineration the logo can be used in different background without losing the forms of the brand.
