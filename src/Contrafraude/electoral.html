    Contrafraude Electoral
    Denounce Illegal Acts

    After the elections that happened in Mexico in 2012, the citizens got some distrust about the transparency that existed during these. With this in mind, our client asked us to create a tool that worked as a link between citizens and authorities to report all illegal or suspicious acts with their cellphone.

    Role

    Digital product designer & Art director


    Deliverables

    Branding
    UI Design


    Team

    Krieger


    Year

    2018

    Problem Overview

    The initial idea for this platform was to solve RH challenges. We started to think from HR perspective of the process they needed to go through to hire a new employee In the previous elections, it was easy to notice that Mexico was disillusioned with the electoral process, they notice a lack of democracy and untrustful politicians.

    After the result was showing of the elected president it was easy to know the different illegal practices that the politics doo to win some votes in a not ethic form and got favourable results at the end of elections. But the elections of 2012 exist a lot of denounces that demonstrate the methods that they use that wen from giving some money to give away some electronic gadgets.

    So, how would we make citizens trust again in the electoral process?



    Process

    The delivery included user experience and interface design for the mobile app for the two principal platforms. So the topmost concern was to create an easy-to-use application that let the citizens recording suspicious activity as soon as it happens and send immediately to the corresponding authorities. The app also integrated a newsfeed and a status that let the citizens know about the different denounces that they send.

    Branding

    1. Logo

    The client give us a blank canvas with just a name to work on this. So we start a work with the storytelling were the principal characteristics was bring "light" in the electional process with a little help of the citizens. So thats why we choose as a principal element a lamp holder that could expose evil-doers. For the color palette I use neutral non-partisan color palette.

    2. Brand Guidelines

    I choose a font that remind us the old-newspaper headlines were we notice big and heavy titles that shows a tradicional and serious impression.

    Understanding the user

    User flows

    I create different flowcharts grouped in the actions for each section and for the different permissions that exist in the platform to show the complete journey that user is going to follow to complete each task that the tool is going to help them.

    Nebvla for business

    Based on the workflows and different kind of sketches that I created in the process, I started in work in the wireframes to create a prototype and test with our principal users to start to identify some problems in the flows and star to make corrections.

    I create at least two different solutions to observe what was better and easier for the user.

    With constant communication & feedback of the users, I created the entire high fidelity design for the web app and mobile app. After that, I create a design system that helps the developers build the final version of the tool and that let us keep growing the tool in the future.

    Final thoughts

    I like to be part of giving a solution of one of the principal problematics that exist in the elections, is an important start to create transparent and legal links between citizens and authorities and give a fast time in the complaint process. Is good to use the technology that we always keep at our hands like is the cellphone to demonstrate the wrong things and start a rethinking in the actual process to start to have democratic and transparent elections in the future.

    At last, I like that client have on mind to get a launch planning of the app and in this order got a bigger diffusion on more people to use the app.
