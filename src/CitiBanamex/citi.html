CitiBanamex

Redesigning core banking journeys, scaling design systems, and driving product discovery

During my time at Globant, I worked on CitiBanamex’s digital banking platform at a moment of transformation.

  • The redesign of BancaNet, CitiBanamex’s core digital banking platform
  • The definition and delivery of a shared design system across teams
  • The ideation of personalized product discovery components

Each initiative focused on improving clarity, trust, scalability, and user engagement in a regulated financial environment.

Role

Product Designer
(UX Strategy, Interaction Design, Design Systems)

Deliverables

Product Strategy
UX Strategy
UI Design

Team

Globant / CitiBanamex

Year

I defined UX strategy and interaction patterns across these streams, aligning closely with product, engineering, and design teams.

Users struggled to understand their financial context, reducing trust and engagement.

Key decisions:

  • Simplified information architecture
  • Contextual product discovery
  • Progressive disclosure
  • Accessibility-first patterns

Closing Reflection

This project reinforced the importance of designing across system, experience, and growth layers simultaneously.
