    Blue State

    Money savings module for citibanamex.

    Citibanamex was looking to create a new and more fresh look and feel for the bank. Inline new modules were proposed for create a better experience for users, one of them was a personalized module for savings.

    Role

    Visual Designer
    UX designer


    Deliverables

    Product strategy
    UX strategy
    UI Design


    Team

    Citibanamex


    Year

    2020

    Challenge

    As a user of the bank, I liked to get better control of my incomes and expenses. I believe the app can help to achieve this in an easy way and show me better ways to use my money maybe in other products inside the bank as investments or create a saving found to accomplish my goals.

    Goal

    Create a personalized module inside the app, where in an easy way, let the user got control over their money and at the same time help the bank to show focused ads or promotions and announce other products that could be helpful for users and they can adopt from here in a short time and without of doleful process.

    Next Steps

    A feature to let know to the others the statistics of their money.

    Process

    Researched competitors

    Discussion with some users about paint points and what are some nice have’s

    Ideation of the user flow

    Create a high fidelity mockups

    Final thoughts

    This was an interesting project because it helps to improve the experience of the web app of Citibanamex, it was important to keep in mind the real clients of citibanamex and potential customers, and give them a tool that helps to get better control in their expenses but in a way that is easy for them to understand and contract new products of citibanamex. At the same time when I was working for this project, the bank start accessibility test an be part of this practice helpt me to show the information in a simple way and think better for a solution.
